A Day Of My Life During MCO

Due to the MCO i was stranded here in UITM campus dorm and unable to go back to my hometown which is in Sabah. During this MCO i got some free time to catch up on my favourite shows and movies on Netflix.Most of the time i spend my time on watching Netflix and palying mobile games to kill the boredom.Beside that playing online mobile games also help me to catch up with my friends who's from overseas.Because of the MCO i cannot be with my family physically but thanks to the technology now i still able to spend my time with them by talking with them through voice call and also video calls.

Since we are not allowed to go out from UITM,we are provided with three meals a day by UITM and we do get enough foods here.With the foods provided,it also help me to reduced my expenses cost here because i don't have to buy food from outside. Thanks to UITM we have been taken good care here and healthy as well.
